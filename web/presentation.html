🚗 What is UniRide?
  • Student-to-student ridesharing platform designed for Switzerland
  • Connects university students as drivers and passengers for intercity trips
  • Focus: budget-friendly, safe & campus-connected travel
  • Provides affordable cross-border trips — CH → Italy, France, Germany
  • Launched to fill a gap in the Swiss market where BlaBlaCar is absent
💬 Core Value Proposition
"Share your journey, split the cost — meet fellow students along the way."
🇨🇭 Market Context
  • Switzerland: 8.7 million population, ~30% are university students or young adults
  • SBB (Swiss Rail) fares among highest in Europe — driving demand for alternatives
  • BlaBlaCar does not operate in Switzerland — clear market gap
  • Growing Gen Z preference for shared, sustainable transport
  • Strong university clusters: Geneva, Lausanne, Zurich, Bern, Basel

Platform Rules
Terms & Conditions
18 / 24
🎓
Student Verification
All users must register with a valid university student email (.edu or institutional). Profiles are verified before access is granted to protect the community.
🔒
Identity & Safety
Government ID verification required for drivers. Passengers must have a completed profile. Background checks encouraged for Gold Tier drivers.
💳
Payment Rules
Payments processed in-app only. No cash transactions. Prices are set by the driver and must comply with UniRide's fare guidelines. UniRide takes 15% platform fee.
❌
Cancellation Policy
Free cancellation up to 2 hours before departure. Late cancellations (under 2h) incur a CHF 3 fee. Gold tier passengers are exempt from all cancellation fees.
⭐
Rating System
All rides are mutually rated (driver ↔ passenger). Profiles below 4.0/5.0 are reviewed automatically. Consistently low-rated users may be suspended.
🛡️
Insurance & Liability
Drivers must hold valid Swiss vehicle insurance. UniRide provides supplemental ride-sharing coverage. UniRide is not liable for personal property loss during trips.
ℹ️ Data Protection: UniRide complies with Swiss FADP and EU GDPR regulations. User location data is only collected during active trips. No data sold to third parties.

Key Conclusions
UniRide — Consumer Information & Decision Channel Mapping
🎯
Clear Market Opportunity
BlaBlaCar's absence from Switzerland leaves a significant gap that UniRide is uniquely positioned to fill with a student-first approach.
📱
Social Media is Critical
Instagram, TikTok, and Word of Mouth drive 78–85% of Gen Z awareness. Digital-first campaigns are non-negotiable for acquisition.
🔒
Safety is Non-Negotiable
IPA analysis reveals Safety & Verification scores highest on importance for both user segments — must be a core product pillar.
🏆
Loyalty Drives Retention
UniPoints loyalty program addresses long-term retention. Eco Bonuses and Campus Perks differentiate from generic ridesharing apps.
📍 Next Steps
Launch beta in Geneva & Lausanne → Collect first 500 user reviews → A/B test loyalty tiers → Expand to Zurich & Bern by Q3 2026
